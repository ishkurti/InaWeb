about me

Ina Shkurti

I am a geographer with over a decade of experience collaborating with communities and organizations across Latin America and Amazonia. My work centers on territorial, cultural, and oral history mapping as tools for supporting land and heritage reclamation and anti-extractive struggles. I specialize in community-based cartographic methods, interactive mapping platforms, and geo-storytelling projects that critically engage with and challenge dominant narratives and representations.

I hold a BA in Geography and a Certificate in Geographic Information Systems from the University of Massachusetts Amherst and am currently pursuing a PhD at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill.

Open to collaborations just about anywhere.
